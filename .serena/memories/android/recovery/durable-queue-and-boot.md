Android runtime recovery now has three persistence layers. `OutboundEnvelopeQueueStore` keeps encrypted outbound envelopes in secure preferences until `BridgeRuntime` can drain them after relay reconnect. `RuntimePreferencesStore` remembers whether the foreground bridge runtime was last running so `AirBridgeBootReceiver` can restart it after `BOOT_COMPLETED` or app replacement when paired credentials still exist. `ClipboardSyncStateStore` persists the last sent/applied clipboard fingerprints so service restarts do not immediately resend the same clipboard payload.
