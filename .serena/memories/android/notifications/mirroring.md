Android notification mirroring now builds a richer encrypted payload. `NotificationPayloadNormalizer` extracts app name, title, subtitle, body, category, channel metadata, and best-effort image assets (`appIcon`, `largeIcon`, `heroImage`). `BridgeRuntime` serializes these into `NotificationPayload` with schema_version 2 and sends them on `application/vnd.airbridge.notification+json`. The relay still stays blind and only forwards the encrypted envelope.
